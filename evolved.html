Evolved

Evolved is a game realised during the first year of my Master's degree. It's development lasted about 4 months.

The player embodies a young human that possess magical capabilities in his quest to deafeat an evil wizard. The game alternates between story levels and procedurally generated ones. Moreover, the game uses geolocation match the game's weather to the player's. The weather then affects the gamplay in various ways (different abilites and monsters, ...). The player can use potions to temporarly change the weather.

  • Technologies : Unity
  • Platform : Mobile
  • Team : 4 programmers
During this project, I learned about procedural terrain generation using Perlin noise and I used various methods in Unity to improve overall performance (Object pooling, chunk loading, ...). The final result is a playble prototype with a tutorial, a random level, a boss level and a last level that is virtually infinite.
